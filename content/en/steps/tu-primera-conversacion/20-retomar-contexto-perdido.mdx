## The model has no memory unless you give it one

Outside of the active conversation buffer, the model remembers nothing. <Character name="vega" /> can't pull up "what we were discussing yesterday". there is no yesterday for her, only the tokens currently in context.

When you come back to a conversation, **you** are the memory. Re-establishing context takes 60 seconds of effort up front and saves five turns of confusion. The shape is always the same:

1. **Who I am.** One sentence, identifying yourself.
2. **What I'm trying to do.** The goal, in one sentence.
3. **What we already decided.** The state the prior conversation left things in.
4. **Where I'm stuck.** The specific blocker.
5. **What I need from you.** The exact next question.

Skip any of these and the model has to guess. Guessing is where hallucination starts.

> This is also the right shape for handing off to a human teammate, a support agent, or a coworker after a long weekend. You're not formatting for the model. you're formatting for *anyone who lost the thread*.
