## The crew is waiting for you

There are no huge teams aboard. there's a compact crew of humanoid robots that learned to coordinate without wasting words. You're the new member, and to be folded in you only need to learn one thing: **how to talk to them**.

Today you meet the four that operate with you from your first shift. Two more. <Character name="orbit" /> and <Character name="hex" />. join later, once you reach their areas (multi-step systems and security).

Each one has a specialty and a way of operating. <Character name="vega" /> welcomes you and teaches. <Character name="atlas" /> signs. <Character name="echo" /> verifies data. <Character name="forge" /> builds the tools the rest use.

> Click on each member on the right to learn their role before we start.

When you're ready, move on. <Character name="vega" /> will take you in the next shift.
