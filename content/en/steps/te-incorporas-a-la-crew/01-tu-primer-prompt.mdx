## Before asking <Character name="vega" /> for anything, sort what you're going to say

<Character name="vega" /> is patient but can't stand sloppy text. A prompt she can work with has **four purposeful layers**. If you can tell them apart, you can adjust each one without messing up the rest.

In this exercise you'll see a prompt that already works. the one we use to have <Character name="vega" /> welcome a new crewmate.

Your job: tag the correct label for each part. Four labels, four parts:

- **Role**. who the model is (here, who <Character name="vega" /> is).
- **Context**. what world data the model needs (who's the new crewmate? what do they already know?).
- **Examples**. how a good response should sound.
- **Format**. the exact shape of the output.

> Tip: if a part could fit more than one label, ask yourself what would happen if you deleted it. The right label is the one that would lose the most if that part were gone.
