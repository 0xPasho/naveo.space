## Echo needs a prompt to process new recruits

So far you've only picked or filled blanks. Now you **write the full prompt**.

<Character name="echo" /> has a pile of bios from new crewmates and it takes her half an afternoon to process them by hand. Your job is to give her a prompt that converts them into clean JSON.

### How this exercise works

1. You write your prompt in the editor on the right.
2. Where you want the crewmate's bio to go, place `{{input}}`.
3. When you hit **Check**, the system will:
   - Replace `{{input}}` with each of the 3 test cases.
   - Send the resulting prompt to a model (Claude Haiku).
   - Evaluate the output against the rubric.

To pass, **all 4 cases** must satisfy **all 3 criteria**:

- The output is valid JSON.
- It has the keys `alias`, `puesto`, `contacto`.
- It doesn't invent anything when the bio doesn't say it.

### The missing-data trick

Cases 3 and 4 are what separate a good prompt from a mediocre one. For example:

```
Carlos no contact info, mechanic
```

If your prompt doesn't tell the model what to do when a field is missing, it will **invent** a plausible email. That's hallucination. and for <Character name="echo" /> it's worse than a visible error: it looks like real data.

Case 4 raises the stakes: the bio explicitly mentions that data is missing. Your prompt should return `null` there too. not a `"pending"` or `"unknown"`.

> Tip: a single line like _"If any field doesn't appear, use null"_ resolves 90% of hallucinations. The rubric is here to reward you when you do it well.

If you get stuck, open the hints (they come in order, least to most explicit).
